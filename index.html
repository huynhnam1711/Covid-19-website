What Is Novel Coronavirus?

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) that causes severe acute respiratory syndrome. The first known case was identified in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

The disease has spread worldwide, resulting in an ongoing pandemic.
